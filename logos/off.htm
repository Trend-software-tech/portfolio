Micro E- Learning

Evidence based research suggest that individuals typically can concentrate for 10-15 minutes at a time, but for some individuals this can feel like a mountain to climb. The benefits of our micro e-learning courses are

  • Multi Sensory Approaches to the Courses
  • A tried and tested framework of
    • 6 Modules
    • The course can be completed in 60 minutes.
  • Able to access anytime, to allow for movement breaks and change in setting as needed.
  • The ability to access in the environment that best suits your needs.

Macro E- Learning

We understand that Micro E-Learning may not meet the the need for knowledge and depth that some individuals want in their learning. That’s why we offer our Macro E-Learning courses, which cover the course topic in great depth. Some of the benefits of our Macro e-learning are;

  • The ability to become consumed by the topic and gain a greater understanding of the subject matter
  • An assessment is included at the end of the course to assess your knowledge and expertise.
  • A great option to support professionals with their continued professional development.
